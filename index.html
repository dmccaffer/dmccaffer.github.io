Age-adjusted D-DIMER

From 18th October, 2021, age-adjusted D-DIMER will be in use within CDDFT.

In order to use age-adjusted D-dimer, the assay itself is changing, as are the normal values. It is pretty simple and is as below:

Old assay:
  • Normal range <230 for all patients
New assay:
  • Normal range <500 up to the age of 50
  • From age 51, normal range = age x 10

Therefore, for age 51, the normal cut off is 510. For age 85, it is 850 – and so on.

The lab will be applying the age-adjustment in their reporting – so the normal value reported will be age adjusted, and the result will only appear in red as abnormal if above the age adjusted value.

Suspected cancer 2 week wait referrals

  1. Suspected diagnosis of malignancy: fill in the 2 week wait referral form
  2. Inform the patient and the leaflet in the same form/ link to be handed out.
  3. Request CT TAP as outpatient under 2 WW if not already done and inform the same to patient.
  4. Also record this in the symphony notes and copy of which can be posted to GP so that the primary care team are aware.
  5. The completed form and symphony notes should be sent to
    • cdda-tr.cancer2WWBookingteam@nhs.net AND
    • cddft.aos@nhs.net AND
    • also CC to UHND ED secretaries cddft.eduhndsecretaries@nhs.net
    This can be uploaded onto symphony as a proof.
